Who was Jeff Buckley?

Jeff Buckley was an American singer, songwriter, and guitarist known for his exceptional voice and emotional depth. He released only one album during his lifetime, Grace (1994), which became one of the most influential records of the 1990s. His rendition of “Hallelujah” is considered one of the most revered interpretations of the song. Despite his short career, cut short by his early death in 1997, Buckley left a lasting legacy and continues to be celebrated for his vocal range, artistic sensitivity, and impact on contemporary music.
